Who are we?

We are a local artisan bakery, that specializes in sourdough, fresh baked good and delicious coffee

Where do our ingredients come from?

We use fresh, local Danish ingredients, bread baked daily and coffee roasted in Denmark

What's our story?

Two friends met in a bakery class, sparked a friendship and turned into a thriving business

What's our mission?

To bake fresh, delicious sourdough and serve great coffee using the best local ingredients

Why choose our sourdough?

Our sourdough is naturally fermented, full of flavor and baked fresh every day
